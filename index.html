Welcome to Matrix Precast Concrete limited

We are a specialist precast concrete company.Our plant is situated in Athi-River, 30km from Nairobi CBD Matrix Precast Concrete Ltd was established in 2014. In our production, we use modern methods and equipment to achieve a consistent excellent quality product. We manufacture two types of concrete products, namely:

  • Prestressed concrete products
  • Precast concrete products.
Our Prestressed concrete products include, Prestressed T-beam which is used together with a concrete filler block to make a beam and block flooring system.
